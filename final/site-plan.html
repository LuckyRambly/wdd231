NERT: Nikoria Expedition Rescue Team

Site Planning Document

Site Name & Branding

NERT: Nikoria Expedition Rescue Team

The name was chosen because it accurately represents a fictional organization in the Pokémon world that focuses on rescues, expeditions, and raids to save civilians and stop threats.

Disclaimer: The Nikoria region, NERT, and the entire site design are original concepts created for this project as an experiment in worldbuilding and branding. Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a communication hub for NERT, offering relevant news from Nikoria such as disasters, raids, advertising supplies, showcasing featured agents, requesting a team of NERT agents, or even joining NERT.

Scenarios

  • Scenary 1: A strategist is looking for an agent with high strength and physical endurance to assist in an earthquake disaster.
  • Scenary 2: A Pokémon trainer wants to create a NERT ID so they can purchase specialized supplies.
  • Scenary 3: A civilian wants to stay informed about the latest events in the region.

Color Scheme

The chosen palette accurately represents the colors of a technological and proactive organization that combats the latest threats. Secondary colors have been chosen for each color to add accents and details.

Primary Red (#EE1515) - Primary color for accents, details and the navd and footer
Secondary Red (#972B2B) - For details
Black (#222224) - background
Steel (#404040) - Borders and details
Silver (#C0C0C0) - Cards
White (#F0F0F0) - Cards and Fonts

Typography

Michroma: Used for headers and titles (H1, H2, H3) and logo to get a sharp and futuristic aspect.

Roboto Condensed: font with good readability in large paragraphs and at a certain distance.
